# Fundraising Domain Map

See `docs/ROADMAP.md` for the staged product roadmap. This document captures the domain areas and safety constraints that all roadmap items share.

## Current MVP

The first agent covers platforms and applications. It tracks opportunities, requirements, deadlines, checklists, drafts, and application status.

## Future System Directions

- Opportunity radar: platforms, bank programs, grants, markets, pro bono platforms, corporate CSR programs, competitions, and events.
- Fund passport: approved facts about mission, programs, reports, legal information, public links, cases, and impact.
- Application and reporting agent: pipeline from discovery to report submission and repeat application.
- B2B agent: companies aligned with mental health, HR/wellbeing, education, IT, medicine, and creative industries.
- Private donor agent: careful communication, gratitude, monthly impact digests, donor retention, and feedback.
- Blogger agent: ambassadors, collaborations, auctions, streams, merch drops, and reputational checks.
- Events and merch agent: markets, unit economics, volunteer shifts, inventory, and post-event reporting.
- Paid services agent: corporate psychology lectures, internships for psychologists, educational products, landing pages, and offer testing.

## Non-Negotiables

- No stigmatizing language.
- No pressure on beneficiaries.
- No personal beneficiary data in LLM prompts.
- Human approval before all external communication.
